CARP Posters

Girl's Skate Clinic Poster

I made this poster. It's about a girls skate clinic. I had to use the Carp Principles. Contrast, alignment, repitition, proximity. For the finished poster, I applied contrast by using black and white for the background and text. In terms of alignment, I made sure all the sides of the text were aligned. When it came to repitition, I used repeated a lot of symbols. Lastly for the proximity part I made sure all of the text were the same difference from each other.
